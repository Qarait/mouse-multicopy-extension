Collect highlighted text into numbered paste slots

Mouse MultiCopy turns repeated research copying into a faster selection flow. Highlight key phrase one, then highlight key phrase two, then highlight key phrase three.

The snippets stay in quick slots, so a user can paste slot 1, 2, 3, or copy everything together without digging through clipboard history.

Text stays local in browser extension storage. The prototype does not send copied text to a server.
