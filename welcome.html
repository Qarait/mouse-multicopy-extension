Mouse MultiCopy is ready

Highlight text on normal webpages and Mouse MultiCopy quietly builds a clean collection. Copy every highlight into Word, Notepad, email, or any other app when you are ready.

Try The Simple Flow

  1. Open a normal webpage with text you can highlight.
  2. Optionally enter the book or document page in the extension popup.
  3. Highlight one phrase, then another phrase, then a third phrase.
  4. Watch the small confirmation show the highlight number and a text preview.
  5. Open the popup and click Copy All.
  6. Paste once anywhere. Page and source details are optional under Options.

More Control When You Need It

  • Open Options to name, reorder, copy, or delete individual highlights.
  • Pause collect mode when you are selecting text but do not want to save it.
  • Undo appears after a capture if you saved something by mistake.
  • Duplicate protection keeps the same selection from filling multiple slots.
  • Minimum character length avoids tiny accidental selections.
  • Sessions keep separate highlight sets for different tasks.

Chrome system pages such as chrome://extensions do not allow extension page widgets.
