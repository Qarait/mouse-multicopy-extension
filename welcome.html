Mouse MultiCopy is ready

Highlight text on normal webpages to collect snippets into numbered slots. Open the floating MC button or the extension popup to paste a specific slot.

Try The Basic Flow

  1. Open a normal webpage with text you can highlight.
  2. Highlight one phrase, then another phrase, then a third phrase.
  3. Click the floating MC button at the bottom-right of the page.
  4. Focus a text box and click slot 1, 2, or 3 to paste that saved text.
  5. Double-click a slot name to rename it, or drag saved slots to reorder them.

Useful Safety Controls

  • Pause collect mode when you are selecting text but do not want to save it.
  • Undo appears after a capture if you saved something by mistake.
  • Duplicate protection keeps the same selection from filling multiple slots.
  • Minimum character length avoids tiny accidental selections.
  • Sessions keep separate slot sets for different tasks.

Chrome system pages such as chrome://extensions do not allow extension page widgets.
